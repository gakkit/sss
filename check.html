4.0 out of 5 starsI like this shelf very much
ByAnnette L.on May 21, 2017
Size: 1 Wall Shelf|Color: Walnut|Verified Purchase
I like this shelf very much, now. However, I had to make some adjustments to it so that is why it lost a star rating. The shelves are laminated particle board and each board has light edges. I think that makes it look cheap so I used a black (or brown) Sharpie pen along every edge. Now it almost looks like real wood. Mine is walnut, but the black one would probably need this done as well. It also comes with raised screw caps, but I only used them on the shelves. On the vertical boards (not the shelves) I used thin self-adhesive screw covers caps. You cannot even notice them now from a few feet away. The little details make all the difference.
